Identification

Identification image placeholder
Add labelled catalogue image at assets/img/identification/angular-contact-ball-bearings.jpg
  • Visual Shape: Similar to a deep groove ball bearing, but with an offset raceway/shoulder profile.
  • Contact Angle: Handles combined radial and axial load through an angled load path.
  • Common Markings: 72xx, 73xx, 70xx, B, BE, AC, C, A, P4/P5 or matched-pair markings.
  • Handling Note: Often supplied or fitted in pairs. Check orientation and preload requirements.
  • Do Not Confuse With: Deep groove ball bearings, which are less directional.

Application

  • Machine Tools: Spindles, rotary tables, and precision machinery.
  • Industrial Machinery: Pumps, compressors, blowers, and gearboxes.
  • Automotive & Transport: Wheel hubs, clutches, and transmissions.
  • High-Speed/Aerospace: Turbine engines, dentistry equipment, and aircraft control systems.
  • Renewable Energy: Wind turbines and generators.
