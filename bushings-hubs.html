Identification

  • Visual Shape: Taper lock, QD or weld-on hubs used to mount pulleys, sprockets or couplings.
  • Key Checks: Confirm shaft bore, keyway, taper system, fixing pattern and hub style.
  • Common Markings: 1008, 1210, 1610, 2012, 2517, 3020 and similar bush sizes.
  • Fitment Note: Bush and mating component must use the same taper system.
  • Do Not Confuse With: Plain bearing bushes.

Application

  • Power Transmission Drives: Used to mount pulleys, sprockets, gears, and couplings onto shafts.
  • Conveyors: Secure drive components on conveyor shafts and rollers.
  • Fans & Blowers: Mount pulleys and drive components for belt-driven systems.
  • Agricultural Equipment: Used where drive components need easy removal and replacement.
  • General Machinery: Provide practical shaft mounting for rotating components without machining custom hubs.
